Welcome to NOMA

Managing your expenses just got easier! NOMA is your go-to solution for straightforward and effective expense tracking. No frills, just a clean and simple way to stay on top of your spending.

Key features:

  • Easy Expense Entry: Quickly log your expenses with just a few clicks. No complex forms or unnecessary details — just the essentials to keep things uncomplicated.
  • Clear Spending Insights: See where your money is going at a glance. Our intuitive charts and reports help you understand your spending patterns without any confusion.
  • Budget Friendly: Set budgets for different categories and track your progress effortlessly. Get notifications when you're nearing your limits to help you stay within your financial goals.

Why Choose NOMA?

  • No Cost, No Ads: NOMA is absolutely free to use, with no hidden costs or annoying advertisements. Enjoy a clean, ad-free experience while you take control of your finances.
  • User-Friendly Design: Our interface is designed with simplicity in mind. No steep learning curve—start tracking your expenses right away.
  • Accessible Anywhere: Whether you're at home or on the go, access your expense data from any device. Your financial information is always at your fingertips.
